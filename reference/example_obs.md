# Example airquality station observations

Example airquality station observations

## Usage

``` r
example_obs
```

## Format

\## \`example_obs\` A tibble with 8,740 rows and 15 columns:

- date_utc:

  Observation time in UTC (time-ending)

- date_local:

  Observation time in local time (time-ending)

- pm25_1hr:

  PM2.5 concentration in micrograms per cubic meter

- pm10_1hr:

  PM10 concentration in micrograms per cubic meter

- o3_1hr:

  O3 concentration in parts per billion

- no_1hr:

  NO concentration in parts per billion

- no2_1hr:

  NO2 concentration in parts per billion

- so2_1hr:

  SO2 concentration in parts per billion

- trs_1hr:

  Total Reduced Sulphur concentration in parts per billion

- rh_1hr:

  Relative Humidity in percent

- temp_1hr:

  Temperature in degrees Celsius

- wd_1hr:

  Wind direction in degrees

- wd_unitvector_1hr:

  Wind direction (unit vector average) in degrees

- ws_1hr:

  Wind speed in meters per second

- ws_vector_1hr:

  Wind speed (vector average) in meters per second
